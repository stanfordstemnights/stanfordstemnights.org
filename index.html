Our Mission

Stanford STEM Nights aims to make STEM more equitable and accessible by hosting evening educational events. During these evenings, we teach hands-on science and engineering activities to students at local underserved elementary schools. More broadly, we aim to connect the Stanford community with local schools and inspire young people to be curious and excited to learn about how the world works.
